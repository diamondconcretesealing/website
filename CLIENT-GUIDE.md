# Updating Your Website

Two parts of your site update without any coding: **Projects** and **Google Reviews**.

---

## Adding a Project Photo + Description

Your project gallery is managed in a simple dashboard called **Sanity Studio**.

1. Go to **`https://YOUR-SITE.com/studio`** (bookmark this).
2. Sign in with the Google account you were invited with.
3. Click **Project** in the left list, then the **pencil / "Create new"** button.
4. Fill in:
   - **Photo** — drag in or upload the picture. After it uploads, type a short **Alt text** describing it (e.g. "Sealed exposed-aggregate driveway"). This is required.
   - **Description** — the caption shown when someone hovers the photo.
   - **Display order** — a number. Lower numbers show first. The very first project is shown larger than the rest.
   - **Title** — optional, just a label for you.
5. Click **Publish** (bottom right).

Your new project appears on the website **within about an hour** (the site refreshes itself on a schedule).

**To edit or remove a project:** open it in the same list, change it and **Publish**, or use the **⋮** menu → **Delete**.

---

## Google Reviews

These are **fully automatic**. The site pulls your real Google reviews through a free service called **Featurable** and refreshes them on its own (about every 2 days).

- **You don't do anything in the website** for reviews to update.
- To get *more* reviews showing, simply collect more Google reviews from happy customers — they'll appear automatically.
- If you ever want to change how the reviews look or which ones show, log into your **Featurable** account (the dashboard you were set up with).
